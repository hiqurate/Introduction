Large enterprises can afford premium consulting. Small and mid-sized businesses cannot.

04 Key Gaps Identified

Low Awareness

Real-world cyber risks are often misunderstood or underestimated across industries.

Optional Mindset

Security is treated as a "nice-to-have" add-on rather than a business-critical function.

Tool-First Vendors

Vendors focus on selling licenses and boxes instead of actually reducing business risk.

No Clear Priority

SMBs lack understanding of what to fix first, leading to paralysis or wasted budget.
